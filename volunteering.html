VOLUNTEERING

FIRST Robotics Competition

Mentor & Alumni

I actively mentor high school students in the FIRST Robotics Competition, guiding them through the engineering design process, fabrication, and programming of competitive robots.

My role involves teaching technical skills in CAD and mechanical assembly, as well as fostering teamwork and problem-solving abilities. Giving back to the program that sparked my interest in engineering is incredibly rewarding.

STEM Outreach

Community Workshop Leader

I organize and lead workshops for local youth to introduce them to aerospace concepts. From bottle rockets to glider workshops, I aim to inspire the next generation of engineers by making science fun and accessible.
