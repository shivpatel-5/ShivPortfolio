About Me

I am a 3rd Year Aerospace Engineering student at Toronto Metropolitan University, passionate about Aerodynamics, CAD Design, and Propulsion Systems. My journey in engineering is driven by a curiosity to understand how things fly and a desire to build the next generation of aerospace vehicles.

When I am not designing in CATIA or SolidWorks, I am actively involved in student leadership, mentoring first-year students, and connecting my peers with industry professionals through CASI.

Professional Experience

Academic Link (Student Advisor)

TMU Residence | Faculty of Engineering & Architectural Sciences

I take pride in serving the TMU Residence community as the Student Advisor, representing the Faculty of Engineering and Architectural Sciences. In this role, I oversee over 200 students in their transition to first year and serve as the primary point of contact for their academic and personal inquiries.


I also take pride in being someone students can confidently approach for guidance, mentorship, and solutions. Through these experiences, I have improved my leadership, problem-solving, and communication skills by carefully assessing the needs of diverse students and providing thoughtful support that fosters both academic success and community well-being while managing my time across courses and extracurricular activities.
