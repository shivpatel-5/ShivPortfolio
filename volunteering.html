Engineering Student Ambassador

FEAS Outreach | FuturENG Mentor

On November 16th, FEAS Outreach hosted the 2024 "FuturENG" event for women in STEM in grades 9-12, supported by HydroOne through the WEMADEIT program. As a mentor, I guided highly motivated female students as they explored the world of engineering through hands-on workshops.

I mentored students in crafting and designing electric vehicles using materials produced in our cutting-edge labs, helping them navigate challenges that arose during manufacturing and design.

Student Ambassador

TMU Open House

As a Student Ambassador during the TMU Open House, I acted as the face of the university for thousands of visitors. I played a key role in creating a welcoming environment for potential applicants and their families.

I guided tours of our engineering facilities, provided detailed information about the aerospace program, and engaged visitors in meaningful conversations about the innovative and inclusive culture at Toronto Metropolitan University.
